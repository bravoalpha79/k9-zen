What we offer

Obedience Class

From leash walking, stopping, heeling and recall to "speaking", staying and releasing items - this course covers everything your pet needs to master for everyday interactions. An essential for any urban dog.

Tricks Class

While the two of you are not expected to perform in a circus group after this course (but you could if you chose to!), tricks training strengthens the owner-dog relationship and enhances your dog's focus and confidence.

Individual Training

You prefer two-on-one interaction rather than group work? We've got you covered! Individual training at our training grounds or in your backyard or neighbourhood park - the choice is all yours.

Consultation

You've encountered a problem and are not sure if it is a problem to begin with? Not sure how to address some specific behaviour of your dog? Or perhaps you need advice on food or training equipment? We're here to support you with our extensive experience.

Please check our location and timetable sections for details about our classes. For information about individual training and consultation, as well as for any other question you might have, please do not hesitate to contact us.
