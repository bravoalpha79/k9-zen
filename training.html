Obedience class

From leash walking, stopping, heeling and recall to "speaking", staying and releasing items - this course covers everything your dog needs to master for everyday interactions.

Duration: 8 weeks (2x / week)
Price: HRK 1500
Tricks class

While the two of you are not expected to perform in a circus group after this course (but you could if you chose to!), tricks training strengthens the owner-dog relationship and enhances your dog's focus and confidence.

Duration: 4 weeks (2x / week)
Price: HRK 1000
Individual training

You prefer two-on-one interaction rather than group work? Or perhaps our class schedule is incompatible with yours? Don't worry, we've got you covered! Individual training at our training grounds or in your backyard or neighbourhood park - the choice is all yours.

Duration: varies
Price: varies
