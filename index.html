Why K9Zen?

Traditional dog training methods rely on the "carrot-and-stick" approach to direct the dog towards desirable behavioural patterns. However, while appearing effective on the short term, any form of punishment may induce unwanted long-term effects on the dog such as development of anxiety or aggression, and negatively impact the owner-dog relationship.

At K9Zen, we apply the Positive Reinforcement Training method. This means that, instead of resorting to "corrections" (punishment) to deter undesired dog reactions, we rely solely on positive motivation (rewarding) to induce desirable behaviour.

Put simply: we want your dog to obey you out of desire, not fear.
